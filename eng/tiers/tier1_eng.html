> Varum (singer) - is Agutin with an auto-tune

The theory that the song "Everything is in your Hands" was actually performed by Agutin has been around for several years. There are videos on YouTube, the authors of which lower Varum's voice by two tones and get a voice very similar to Agutin. An example with "Everything is in your hands", as well as with Varum's song "The Artist who draws Rain", Dudu was shown in 2017 by his guest, musician Neuromonk Feofan. In both tracks they heard Agutin's voice. Andrey Malakhov is also confident in the substitution.
Agutin himself claims that he has nothing to do with it. He explained to Dudu that the first song was recorded in 1997, and the second one was recorded back in 1991, when he was not familiar with Varum. In those days, the musician said, it was not yet possible to change the tonality of the voice without changing the speed: songs were written on tape, and raising the tonality would increase the tempo.

My Verdict:
Judging by various information, analysis of Varum and Agutin's songs, as well as their voices, it can be said that with great probability Agutin sang for Varum using a light autotune. However, Agutin still proves the opposite to this day.

> Lakhtobots

The Internet Research Agency (also referred to in the press and other sources as "kremlebots", "troll factory", "Prigozhin trolls", "Olginsky trolls", "lakhtobots") is a Russian structure with headquartered in St. Petersburg, engaged in disinformation, the formation of public opinion, in particular the discrediting of the Russian opposition. The agency uses fake accounts in major social networks and online publications, forums and video hosting to promote the interests of the customer, mainly engaged in spreading the pro-Kremlin point of view in domestic and foreign policy (including the armed conflict in eastern Ukraine and the civil war in Syria).
According to the investigation by US Special Prosecutor Robert Mueller, the organization is sponsored by the companies of Russian businessman Yevgeny Prigozhin, known as "Putin's cook".

My Verdict:
Lakhtobots - the generalized name of all bots and troll groups.
